Avocado White Bean Sandwich

Description:

This quick and nutrient packed sandwich is great for an on-the-go lunch. This recipe only takes about 5 minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Avocado, quartered
  • 1/2 Lime, juice of
  • 1/2 of 15 oz can White beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 Cucumber, sliced
  • 2 Tbsp cilantro lime hummus (or flavor of choice)
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic to taste
  • 4 slices whole wheat bread (or bread of choice)
  • 1 handful Spinach

Directions:

  1. Toast bread
  2. Pour white beans into a medium bowl and mash a little with a potato masher
  3. Add in the avocado and lime juce, mash until just a few chunks of bean and avocado are left
  4. Stir in the salt, pepper, and garlic to taste
  5. Spread hummus onto the toast
  6. Scoop the mix onto the bread
  7. Top with cucumbers, spinach, and other desired toppings
