Black Bean Ceviche

Description:

This vegan take on ceviche is more of a black bean salad - the most delicious salad you can eat! The black beans "cook" in the lime juice and come out deliciously tart. Enjoy on a tortilla, lettuce wrap, chips, or by the spoonful! This 20 minute recipe will become a staple in your home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 15 oz can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 small limes
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 3 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 large avocado, diced
  • 3 green onions, sliced thin
  • 1 handful cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprik
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Pour drained black beans into a bowl. Squeeze limes over top and mix to coat. Allow to sit for about 15 minutes.
  2. While the beans soak, add corn kernels to a medium saucepan, and cook over medium heat until it starts to get brown and caramelized. Remove from heat and allow to cool.
  3. Add the corn, tomatoes, avocado, green onions, cilantro, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to the beans. Mix to combine thoroughly.
