Sweet Potato Chickpea Bowl

Description:

Did someone say Buddha bowl? This collection would not be conplete without at least one Buddha bowl recipe. This mix of nutrient dense veggies is so warm and comforting you almost forget that it is good for you! 30 minutes and counting until you are sitting down to devour this flavorful bowl.

Ingredients:

    Vegetables

  • 2 Tbsp olive oil (or oil of choice)
  • 1/2 medium red onion, sliced in wedges
  • 2 small sweet potatoes, halved
  • 1 bundle broccolini, chopped
  • 2 big handfuls kale leaves
  • 1/4 tsp each salt and pepper

    • Chickpeas

  • 1 15 oz can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 3/4 tsp chili powder
  • 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp each salt and pepper

    • Tahini Sauce

  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  • 1/2 medium lemon
  • 2-4 Tbsp hot water

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F
  2. Arrange sweet potatoes and onions on a bare baking sheet. Drizzle both with a bit of oil, making sure the flesh of the sweet potatoes are well coated and placed skin side down on the sheet.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from oven flip sweet potatoes and add broccolini. Drizzle broccolini with a bit of oil and season with a pinch each salt and pepper.
  4. Bake for another 8-10 minutes, then remove from oven and add kale. Drizzle kale with a touch more oil and season with a pinch each salt and pepper. Bake for another 4-5 minutes then set aside.
  5. While vegetables are roasting, heat a large skillet over medium heat and add chickpeas to a medium mixing bowl and toss with seasonings.
  6. Once hot, add 1 Tbsp oil and chickpeas to the skillet and sauté, stirring frequently. About 10 minutes.
  7. Once the chickpeas are browned and fragrant, remove from heat and set aside.
  8. Prepare sauce by adding tahini, maple syrup, and lemon juice to a small mixing bowl and whisking to combine. Add hot water until a pourable sauce is formed. Set aside.
  9. To serve: slice sweet potatoes into bite size pieces. Divide vegetables between 3 serving bowls and top with chickpeas and tahini sauce.
